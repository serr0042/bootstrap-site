Top Three Teams in the League

Fitchburg Finches

Massachusetts

The Fitchburg Finches are based in Fitchburg, Massachusetts, USA. They have won the United States League seven times and are currently at the top of the World League with 1050 points. They will be playing against the Braga Broomfleet in the World Quidditch League Final next week.

Braga Broomfleet

Portugal

Braga Broomfleet are based in Braga, Portugal. They became one of the best in the league using a Beater-marking system they invented. They face off against the Fitchburg Finches in the World Quidditch League Final.

Appleby Arrows

England

The Appleby Arrows are based in Appleby, a town in northern England. They play in robes of pale blue with a silver arrow emblazoned on them. There is a fierce rivalry between them and the Wimbourne Wasps.
